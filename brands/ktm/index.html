KTM Vehicle Categories

Whatever your terrain, there's a KTM engineered to dominate it.

SX

Motocross — SX / XC

SX 50, SX 65, SX 85, SX 125, SX 150, SX 250, SX-F 250/350/450 — the most championship-proven motocross bikes in the sport.

EXC

Enduro — EXC / XC-W

EXC 150, EXC 250, EXC-F 250/350/500, XC-W series — the world's best enduro machines, straight from factory to trail.

Duke

Street — Duke Series

125 Duke, 390 Duke, 790 Duke, 890 Duke, 1290 Super Duke R — the most aggressive naked streetfighters available.

RC

Sport — RC Series

RC 125, RC 390, RC 8C — full fairing sport bikes with MotoGP DNA brought to the street and track.

ADV

Adventure — ADV Series

390 Adventure, 790 Adventure, 890 Adventure R, 1090/1190/1290 Super Adventure — go further, go harder.

SMC

Off-Road / Supermoto

Freeride, Supermoto, and Hardenduro models — KTM's pure off-road and dual-sport DNA in dedicated machines.
