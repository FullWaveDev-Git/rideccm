Serving Clark County

Clinton County Motorsports — Serving Springfield Riders

Springfield and Clark County riders — whether you're in New Carlisle, Enon, South Vienna, or Tremont City — will find that Clinton County Motorsports in Wilmington is well worth the roughly 50-minute drive southeast. Take US-35 E through Xenia and drop south on US-68 to reach us at I-71 Exit 50 in Wilmington.

With 12+ authorized brands including KTM, Husqvarna, Gas Gas, Yamaha, Kawasaki, Polaris, Can-Am, Triumph, Suzuki, Slingshot, and Can-Am Spyder, CCM is the most complete powersports dealership within reach of Springfield. Our full-service department handles everything from routine maintenance to major engine work, and our parts staff can source any OEM component you need.

Whether you ride dirt, street, or trails — or need a side-by-side for your property — CCM has the inventory, expertise, and financing to get you set up.
