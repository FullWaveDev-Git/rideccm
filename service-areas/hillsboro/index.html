Serving Highland County

CCM — Hillsboro's Powersports Destination

Hillsboro and Highland County are home to some of Ohio's best riding country — rolling hills, quiet back roads, and trails near Rocky Fork State Park and Paint Creek State Park make this area a powersports paradise. Clinton County Motorsports in Wilmington is the closest authorized multi-brand powersports dealer for Hillsboro, Lynchburg, Greenfield, and all of Highland County.

We're directly north on US-68 — it's a straight, 25-mile shot that takes about 25 minutes. No interstates, no complexity. Just follow US-68 North and you'll arrive at CCM on the north side of Wilmington at I-71 Exit 50.

With authorized brands including KTM, Yamaha, Polaris, Can-Am, Kawasaki, Triumph, Suzuki, Husqvarna, Gas Gas, Slingshot, and Can-Am Spyder — plus factory-certified service and a fully stocked parts department — CCM is everything a Highland County rider needs under one roof.
