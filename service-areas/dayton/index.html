Serving the Dayton Metro

Clinton County Motorsports — Dayton's Powersports Dealer

Dayton riders — including those in Kettering, Centerville, Miamisburg, Beavercreek, Fairborn, Huber Heights, Trotwood, and throughout Montgomery and Greene Counties — trust Clinton County Motorsports as their go-to authorized powersports dealer. We're located just 35 miles south of Dayton in Wilmington, OH, making us an easy 40-minute drive down US-35 or via I-75 South to US-68.

With one of the largest selections of new and pre-owned motorcycles, ATVs, UTVs, and side-by-sides in the region — plus factory-certified technicians and a fully stocked parts department — CCM is the destination Dayton powersports enthusiasts come back to again and again.

We carry brands no other dealer in the immediate Dayton area offers, including KTM, Husqvarna, Gas Gas, and Triumph alongside Yamaha, Kawasaki, Polaris, Can-Am, and Suzuki.
