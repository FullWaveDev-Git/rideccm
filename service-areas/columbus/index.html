Serving Central Ohio

Clinton County Motorsports — Columbus' Powersports Dealer

Columbus riders from Dublin, Grove City, Hilliard, Westerville, Pickerington, Lancaster, and throughout Franklin and Fairfield Counties make the trip to Clinton County Motorsports for the region's best selection of new and pre-owned powersports vehicles. We're about 60 miles southwest of Columbus — easy access via US-35 West or I-71 South to Exit 50.

CCM offers brands that many Columbus-area dealers simply don't carry: KTM, Husqvarna, Gas Gas, Sherco, and Triumph alongside household names like Yamaha, Kawasaki, Polaris, Can-Am, and Suzuki. If you're serious about powersports, the drive to Wilmington is absolutely worth it.

Our factory-certified technicians, fully stocked parts department, and flexible financing options make CCM the complete powersports destination for Central Ohio riders.
