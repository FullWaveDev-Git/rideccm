Is there a motorcycle dealer near Columbus, Ohio?

Yes — Clinton County Motorsports in Wilmington, OH is the closest authorized multi-brand motorcycle and powersports dealer to Columbus. We're just 60 miles away via I-71 South to Exit 50 or US-35 West, about ~60 minutes of driving. We carry Yamaha, Kawasaki, KTM, Triumph, Suzuki, Husqvarna, Gas Gas, and more.

Where can Columbus riders find a dirt bike or off-road motorcycle dealer?

CCM is an authorized KTM, Husqvarna, Gas Gas, and Yamaha dealer — all top brands for off-road, motocross, and enduro riding. If you're looking for a dirt bike shop near Columbus, we're ~60 minutes away and carry a wide selection of new and pre-owned off-road machines.

Does CCM have ATV and side-by-side dealers near Columbus?

Absolutely. We're an authorized Polaris, Can-Am, and Yamaha dealer for ATVs and UTVs. Whether you're looking for a sport quad, a utility ATV, a recreational side-by-side, or a work UTV, CCM carries current models with full warranty support and factory-certified service.

CCM's service department is staffed by factory-certified motorcycle mechanics for every brand we carry. Columbus riders can call ahead to schedule service at (937) 283-2220.

We're open Tuesday–Friday 10am–6pm and Saturday 9am–3pm, closed Sunday and Monday. Our address is 6002 U.S. 68 N, Wilmington, OH 45177 — right at I-71 Exit 50, 60 miles from Columbus. Call or text us at (937) 283-2220.
