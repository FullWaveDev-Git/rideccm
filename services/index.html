Good to Know

Service Policies

Please review our service department policies before bringing in your vehicle

Vehicle Age Policy

We service motorcycles up to 15 years old and ATVs/SXS up to 12 years old. Exceptions may be made at the Service Manager's discretion. Vehicles purchased from CCM receive 3 years of continued service regardless of age.

Parts Installation

We only install parts purchased through Clinton County Motorsports — with the exception of tires. This ensures quality and allows us to stand behind all work performed.

Payment Policy

A 3% credit card convenience fee applies to credit card payments. This fee does not apply to cash or debit card transactions.
