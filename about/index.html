Our Story

Welcome to Clinton County Motorsports

Welcome to Clinton County Motorsports, your premier authorized retailer for Yamaha, Suzuki, Kawasaki, KTM, Polaris, Slingshot, Triumph, Husqvarna, Can-Am, Sherco, and GasGas powersports in Wilmington, OH.

Clinton County Motorsports provides superior customer service and has an entire team that consists of professional sales members, finance experts, service technicians, and a knowledgeable parts and accessories staff to support you.

We pride ourselves on having well-trained experts that strive for customer satisfaction. Come in and visit our friendly, reliable, and experienced staff — or call and text us at (937) 283-2220.
