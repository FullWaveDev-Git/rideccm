Know Before You Go

Service Policies

Please review our service policies before scheduling your appointment.

Vehicle Age Policy

Motorcycles: maximum 15 years old. ATVs & Side-by-Sides: maximum 12 years old. Exception: vehicles purchased from CCM receive a 3-year extension regardless of age.

Parts Installation Policy

CCM installs only parts purchased through CCM. Exception: tires — we will mount carry-in tires for a standard mounting fee.

Diagnosis Deposit

A diagnosis deposit is required for all non-warranty repair work before diagnosis begins. This deposit is applied toward the total repair cost.

Pickup & Delivery

Pickup and delivery service available. Minimum charge of $99 or $2.85 per loaded mile (one way), within a 1.5-hour maximum travel radius from our Wilmington location.

Payment & Credit Card Fee

A 3% convenience fee is applied to all credit card transactions, effective March 1, 2025. Cash and check payments accepted with no surcharge.
